# Enrichment Research

## Stage Contract
- Required Input: `01-study-notes.md` and `02-insights.md`.
- Optional Input: SERP results, Google Scholar, government reports, industry whitepapers, news articles, competitor PR campaigns, blog articles, US local newspapers, and local USA newspaper sources.
- Output: source-backed context, comparators, timing hooks, risks, contradictions, and claims to avoid.
- Handoff: send to `angle-generator`.
- Validation: each research claim needs source, credibility score, date/timeframe, geography when relevant, and limitation.
- Selected Angle Status: not applicable at this stage.
- Source Rule: prioritize authoritative sources and mark unverifiable context as `Information unavailable. Verification required before use.`
- Credibility Scale: `1 = weak/not suitable for claims`; `2 = background only`; `3 = acceptable/use cautiously`; `4 = strong/supporting claims`; `5 = authoritative/ideal for final claims`.
