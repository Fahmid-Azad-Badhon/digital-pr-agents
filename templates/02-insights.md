# Insights

## Stage Contract
- Required Input: `01-study-notes.md`, raw source evidence, methodology, caveats.
- Optional Input: external comparators, user priority findings, local or county breakdowns.
- Output: ranked insight table with finding, evidence, value, novelty, caveat, and source basis.
- Handoff: send to `03-research.md` and `04-angles.md` after research enrichment.
- Validation: no insight may advance without evidence, source context, timeframe/geography where applicable, and a limitation note.
- Selected Angle Status: not applicable at this stage.
- Source Rule: unsupported numbers must be removed or marked `Information unavailable. Verification required before use.`

## Data Point Integrity Checklist
Before any statistic, ranking, percentage, comparison, or date can move downstream, it must answer:
- What is the number?
- What exactly does it measure?
- What timeframe does it cover?
- What geography does it apply to?
- What source supports it?
- What does it prove?
- What does it not prove?
- What limitation should be respected?

Reject, downgrade, or mark `Information unavailable. Verification required before use.` when a claim has a missing source, missing timeframe, missing geography, unsupported comparison, unclear methodology, exaggerated wording, or correlation written as causation.
