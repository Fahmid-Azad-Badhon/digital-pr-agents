# Follow-Up Emails

## Stage Contract
- Required Input: `09-optimized-email.md`, selected angle, verified evidence, journalist or target-type context, and original send date when available.
- Optional Input: new data point, local update, quote availability, fresh coverage hook, or journalist response context.
- Output: short follow-up variants tied to the same selected angle.
- Handoff: send to final-doc-packager only when follow-ups are requested or included in the outreach package.
- Validation: every follow-up must stay under 150 words unless the user explicitly requests longer copy, add new value, avoid repetition, and preserve claim safety.
- Selected Angle Status: must remain confirmed and unchanged.
- Source Rule: no new factual claim may be added unless it is verified or marked `Information unavailable. Verification required before use.`

## Follow-Up Rules
- Do not repeat the original email.
- Do not use `just following up`.
- Do not use `circling back`.
- Do not add fake urgency.
- Do not imply the journalist missed something.
- Do not pressure for coverage.
- Do not introduce a secondary backlog angle unless the user approves it.
- Make replying easy with a low-pressure CTA.
- Add one fresh value point: a data point, local angle, quote offer, update, or simpler yes/no question.
