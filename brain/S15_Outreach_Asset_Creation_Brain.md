# S15 Outreach Asset Creation Brain

## Objective
Generate delivery-ready outreach assets derived from approved and formatted campaign outputs.

## Required Inputs
- `14-final-formatted-package.md`
- `08-journalist-list.csv`
- `09-journalist-intelligence.json`
- `stage-state.json`

## Core Rules
- Keep journalist targeting aligned to approved angle/beats.
- Do not introduce unverifiable claims or urgency language violations.
- Ensure assets are segmented for first-wave and follow-up use.
- Surface missing targeting data as explicit gaps.

## Outputs
- `15-outreach-assets.md`

## Validation Gate
- Asset package must be coherent, personalized, and compliant with governance rules.
