# S16 Campaign Learning Loop Brain

## Objective
Capture campaign outcomes, failures, and optimization insights for continuous improvement.

## Required Inputs
- `13-validation-report.json`
- `14-final-formatted-package.md`
- `15-outreach-assets.md`
- `audit-log.json` (if present)
- `errors.json` (if present)
- `stage-state.json`

## Core Rules
- Log measurable outcomes and unresolved risks.
- Map failures to stage IDs and root causes.
- Record actionable improvements for next campaigns.
- Keep retrospective factual and non-speculative.

## Outputs
- `16-campaign-learning-log.json`

## Validation Gate
- Learning log must include summary, defects, corrective actions, and rerun recommendations.
