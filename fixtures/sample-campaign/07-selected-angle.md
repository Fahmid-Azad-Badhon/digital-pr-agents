# Selected Angle - AI Assistant Launch

## Selected Angle

**Angle 2: The Data Angle**

**Title:** "Small businesses lose $4,500/year to administrative inefficiency - AI can help"

---

## Selection Rationale

This angle was selected because:

1. **Data-driven** - Provides concrete statistics that journalists can use
2. **Broad appeal** - Affects every small business owner
3. **Newsworthy** - First-of-its-kind survey data on SMB AI adoption
4. **Actionable** - Clear solution presented (SmartAssist)

---

## Selected Angle Details

- **Category:** Data/Statistics
- **Target Media:** Business, Tech
- **Best Hook:** "A new survey reveals that the average small business owner spends 10+ hours per week on administrative tasks that could be automated."
- **Key Stat:** 78% of SMB owners want AI help but don't know where to start
- **Product Mention:** SmartAssist ($29/month)
