# Campaign Brief: AI Assistant Launch

## Campaign Overview

**Campaign Name:** AI Assistant Launch  
**Client:** TechStartup Inc.  
**Launch Date:** 2026-06-15  
**Budget:** PR outreach only

## Product Description

A new AI assistant app that helps small business owners manage their schedules, emails, and customer relationships through natural language commands.

## Target Audience

- Small business owners (1-10 employees)
- Solopreneurs
- Freelancers in service industries

## Key Messages

1. **Time Saver:** Save 5+ hours per week on administrative tasks
2. **Easy to Use:** No technical training required - just talk naturally
3. **Affordable:** $29/month - fraction of hiring an assistant
4. **Privacy First:** Your data stays on your device

## Compelling Angles

### Data Angle
- Survey: 78% of small business owners spend 10+ hours/week on admin tasks
- Stat: Average small business loses $4,500/year to inefficiency

### Human Interest Angle
- Story: How a bakery owner uses AI to handle orders while decorating cakes
- Quote: "I went from drowning in emails to having a virtual assistant"

### Industry Trend Angle
- Analysis: The rise of "micro-assistants" for solopreneurs
- Expert: Interview with productivity researcher at Stanford

### Comparison Angle
- vs. Traditional VA: 90% cost savings
- vs. Competitors: Only solution with on-device privacy

## Target Media

- TechCrunch, Wired, Ars Technica (mainstream tech)
- Small Business publications (Trade)
- Local business journals (Regional)

## Success Metrics

- 5+ tier-1 placements
- 10,000 app downloads in first month
- Feature in "Best AI Tools for Small Business" roundups

## Budget Notes

- No paid media
- Focus on earned coverage
- Embargo possible for select outlets
